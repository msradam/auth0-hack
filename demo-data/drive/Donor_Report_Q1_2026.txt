QUARTERLY REPORT TO AMBARA DEVELOPMENT FUND — Q1 2026
Programme: Emergency Response — Post-Cataclysm Waqwaq Reconstruction
Reporting Period: January 1 – March 31, 2026

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Beneficiaries served: 12,400 individuals across three sites
- Kanbaloh: 5,200 individuals (1,040 households)
- Sofala Village: 3,100 individuals (620 households)
- Vakwa Shelter: 4,100 individuals (820 households)

Distribution sites operational throughout the quarter with no significant interruptions.
Flooding zones and unexploded ordnance continue to restrict access to northern routes.

KEY OUTCOMES

1. Food Security: 98.2% distribution completion rate
2. Health: 2,340 medical consultations provided (respiratory illness, frostbite, trauma)
3. Protection: 47 GBV cases identified and referred
4. Shelter: 1,890 displaced persons resettled in reconstructed Sofala Village homes
