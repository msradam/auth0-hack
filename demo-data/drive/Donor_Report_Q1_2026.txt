QUARTERLY REPORT TO HATENO DEVELOPMENT FUND — Q1 2026
Programme: Emergency Response — Post-Upheaval Hyrule Reconstruction
Reporting Period: January 1 – March 31, 2026

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Beneficiaries served: 12,400 individuals across three sites
- Lookout Landing: 5,200 individuals (1,040 households)
- Lurelin Village: 3,100 individuals (620 households)
- Gerudo Shelter: 4,100 individuals (820 households)

Distribution sites operational throughout the quarter with no significant interruptions.
Gloom contamination zones continue to restrict access to northern routes.

KEY OUTCOMES

1. Food Security: 98.2% distribution completion rate
2. Health: 2,340 medical consultations provided (Gloom sickness, frostbite, trauma)
3. Protection: 47 GBV cases identified and referred
4. Shelter: 1,890 displaced persons resettled in reconstructed Lurelin Village homes
